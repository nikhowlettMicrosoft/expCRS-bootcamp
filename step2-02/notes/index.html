Setup WebJob

Setup
  1. Ensure the Service Bus Connection String is set in app.config
  2. Ensure the topic and subscription values in Receiver.cs match the available topics in ServiceBusExplorer
  3. Right click on the WebJob in the Solution Explorer and Build
To actually send and receive data we will need to configure what is known as Role Assignments to authenticate from our dev environemnt
  • Access the Service Bus Resource in Azure Portal
    • This will be the same Service Bus that is listed in the App.config for your WebJob.
  • Enter the Access control (IAM) blade
  • Add a role assignment for Service Bus Data Owner
    • You can now run your WebJob by setting your WebJob as Startup Project and then running, or right-clicking and then debugging the WebJob.
